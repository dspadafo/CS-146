Welcome to Garden

Garden is an app that helps you stay on task by using a Pomodoro timer with plant growth so you can check long term progress

Pomodoro Timer

Complete a focus session to help your garden grow.

Features

Focus Sessions

Use a 25-minute study session layout based on the Pomodoro technique to stay committed to a specific amount of time

Plant Growth Motivation

Every completed study cycle contributes to the growth of your virtual plants, allowing you to see progress in real time

Plant Gallery

Unlock new plants over time and start to build a collection

Progress Tracking

The design of this app encourages you to come back regularly and see your progress

Why This App Matters

Many people lose motivation, especially students when trying to stay on task with long assignments. Regular timers feel boring and repetitive — this is a new way to visualize your actual goals and growth.
