Welcome to Garden Focus

Garden Focus is a study productivity app concept that helps students stay on task by combining a Pomodoro timer with plant growth and long-term progress tracking.

Features

Focus Sessions

Use a 25-minute study session layout based on the Pomodoro technique to stay committed to a specific period of focused work.

Plant Growth Motivation

Every completed study cycle contributes to the growth of your virtual plants, giving students a visible reward for staying productive.

Plant Gallery

Unlock new plants over time and build a collection that reflects your long-term effort.

Progress Tracking

The design of this app encourages students to return regularly and maintain consistent study habits.

Why This App Matters

Many students lose motivation when working on long assignments because regular timers feel repetitive and do not provide a real sense of progress. Garden Focus addresses this problem by turning study sessions into something visual, rewarding, and engaging.
